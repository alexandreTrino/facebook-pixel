#### Facebook Pixel
#### App for tracking the activities of visitors to your store. Tool used to evaluate the effectiveness of your ads.

When you install the app, you will have the following features:

- Integration of your VTEX store with Facebook Analytics.
- A new way to analyze your audience.

To see all the features that we are working on [access the changelog](https://github.com/vtex-apps/facebook-pixel/blob/master/CHANGELOG.md)

Learn more about the Store App [on the Github README.](https://github.com/vtex-apps/facebook-pixel/blob/master/README.md)
